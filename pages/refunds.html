Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

1. General Policy

At FlixoraPlay, we strive to ensure a smooth experience for both competition hosts and creators. Because our platform locks funds securely to guarantee payouts to winners, our refund policy is strict to protect the integrity of the competitions.

2. Host Prize Pool Funding

Once a prize pool is funded by a host via Razorpay, the funds are irrevocably committed to the prize pool of the respective competition. Prize pool funding is strictly non-refundable unless the competition is cancelled by FlixoraPlay due to a violation of our Terms of Service or unforeseen platform errors.

3. Cancellation of Competitions

Hosts may cancel a competition only before the prize pool is funded. Once funded, the competition must run its course, and the prize must be awarded to the winning entries.

4. Processing Errors

If you believe you were charged in error (e.g., duplicate charges), please contact our support team immediately at adithyaacharya710@gmail.com. Erroneous charges will be refunded to the original payment method within 5-7 business days upon verification.

# FlixoraPlay - Platform Overview

FlixoraPlay is an AI-generated video competition and community platform where brands, creators, and normal hosts converge.

## The Core Concept
The platform serves three main user groups:
1. **Competitors (Creators):** AI video enthusiasts can join competitions, showcase their generative AI skills (using tools like Midjourney, Runway, Pika, etc.), earn prizes, and climb the global leaderboard to build an industry-recognized portfolio.
2. **Hosts (Individuals/Agencies):** Anyone can host a competition to crowdsource AI content, whether for fun, to judge creative potential, or to discover new talent for their projects.
3. **Brands (Sponsors):** Companies can issue "Brand Briefs" to source commercial-grade AI videos, crowdsourcing multiple variations of ads from top creators while offering high-value bounties.

## Key Mechanisms
* **Competitions & Briefs:** The core unit is a competition. It has rules, themes, deadlines, and prizes.
* **Entries & Voting:** Creators submit videos. Entries can be judged manually by hosts or via community voting (Elo matchups).
* **Global Leaderboard:** Points are aggregated based on competition wins and engagement, providing a verifiable ranking of the world's best AI creators.
